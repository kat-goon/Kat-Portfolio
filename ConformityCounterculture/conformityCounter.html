Projects About Resume

Conformity & Counterculture

Experimental Collage

Timeline

2023

Overview

A diptych poster series depicting prominent contributers of counterculture and how unassuming they appear if you were to pass them on the street.

Concept Development

Context

The posters tell the story of the United States from 1960 to the mid-70s where counterculture -- a movement that advocated for individuality in society and conformity where people adhered to the "American Dream." While researching, I came to the realization that both parties, in their own way, are fighting for their futures. This duality yet contrast in ideals made me think of the present. Focusing on the activism aspect of conformity and counterculture, I aimed to incite critical thought into the viewers. While they take in the information presented, I want their final sentiment to be “Was the American dream the ‘right’ way to live?” and “How much has changed since then?”

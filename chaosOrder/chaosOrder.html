Overview

A poster series investigating the relationship between chaos and order, using experimental and conventional ways of making.

Imagery

Consuming Conveinence

Everything is a mix of chaos and order. Where we find chaos, there is order and where we find order, there is chaos. Being a foundation of storytelling, I look into how we can create design from chaos and order. By using a ziplock bag with cut out letters jumbled inside, I introduce chaos. Overlaying typography which adheres to a grid brings in the order. Using a mix of analog and digital means, I created a poster series that depicts the relationship between the two and how they co-exist with each other.

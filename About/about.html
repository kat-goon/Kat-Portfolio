Hello there!

Empathy, Collaboration, and storytelling are the core of my design practice. Love for being involved, building community, connecting with people builds empathy. Collaboration - always open to new ideas and feedback. Storytelling - how to interact and leaving a message that resonates with the user.

An experience between universities globally

International Collaboration

In my last year at OCAD University, I had the opportunity to join the International Collaboration Network. A program which covers two semesters and accumulates into an exhibition at a select university. I had the privilege to work with students from universities in Germany, Finland, Mexico, and Japan on various projects.

Main Project

Expression in a Noisy World

The main project I worked on was a collaborative animation called “Expression in a Noisy World.” Through various Zoom calls, we found that we all have a shared interest in animation and were curious about what drives artists to express and create work. Through weekly meetings and setting goals, we were able to complete an animated collage that expresses our individual voices yet adds to a broader conversation on the art world.

Made in collaboration with Wendolyn Fonesca, Nina Lahti, Ivette Perez, Kristal Rico, Ximena Rivera , Matilde Sousa, and Jason Wang.

MexiCanada

Forming connections across the continent

The Winter semester fostered a collaboration between students in Mexico and Canada. For the duration of this project, we worked together to create collage portraits that capture the essence of who we are.
Made in collaboration with Gigliola Durante, Gustavo Antunez, and Ashley Kim

Imagery

Consuming Conveinence

To determine the visuals, I looked into what the Toyota 86 is well known for. Being a rear-wheel-drive car, it is well known for being used for drifting. On the more technical side, this means the 86 has the mechanics to be able to lose traction yet maintain control while going around corners. To mimic this, I scanned images of the cars in a drift track pattern. While it wasn't exactly successfult in caputring drift track patterns, the images produced show movement which was one of the goals.

The Final

The Next Generation

The final is a 20 page editorial that attracts the viewer through movement and form -- elements that often garner people's attention to cars. The target audience being the next generation of car enthusiasts, this catalogue teaches the viewers about the evolution but can also be used as a colouring book to incite dreams of owning the Toyota 86.

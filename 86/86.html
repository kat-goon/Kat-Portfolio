Overview

An expressive catalog on the evolution of Toyota’s beloved sports car. Aimed at new and young car enthusiasts.

The Problem

Consuming Conveinence

Plastic use has become a rising topic through the lens of climate change and environmental sustainability, with Governments becoming increasingly involved in regulating the use of plastic utensils. Yet single plastic use is still extremely high and is still more popular than environmentally friendly options.

Our goal for this research assignment was to find and uncover the usage of single use utensils and the material they’re made of. We wanted to show a relation/comparison between the uses of plastic and wood utensils and to see if there was a discrepancy between the uses of certain material to what the food court offers.

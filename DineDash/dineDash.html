Primary Research, Visual Design, User Flows

Team

Katherine Mendonca

Timeline

3 weeks, 2024

Overview

A game board in which consumers at the Yorkdale Mall food court can interact with. Meant to be an immersive experience, we want to engage our viewers to actively participate in how plastic use can set us back, whereas more environmentally friendly options can help us move forward. Dine & Dash helps to lightly reflect these setbacks and advancements by assigning negative consequences to plastic and positive implications for wood usage.
